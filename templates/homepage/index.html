What is Predict ?

Predict is an online tool that helps patients and clinicians to check their disease and consult with doctors It might have happened so many times that you or someone of yours need doctor’s help immediately, but they are not available due to some reason. The Health Predictor System is an end user support and online consultation project. The system allows the patient to consult the doctor at their convenience by sitting at home.

What does Predict do?

Predict asks for some details about the patient and the cancer. It then uses data about the survival of similar women in the past to show the likely proportion of such women expected to survive up to fifteen years after their surgery with different treatment combinations.

Who is Predict for?

Predict is for clinicians, patients and their families. Patients should use it in consultation with a medical professional.
